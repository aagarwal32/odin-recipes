Fluffy Pancakes

Description

There is nothing more satisfying than taking a bite out of a fluffy pancake. But before we start, how do these pancakes get so fluffy in the first place? Well, the answer is air! Homemade buttermilk from a mix of milk and sugar and two leaveners create air bubbles in the pancake batter which result in light and fluffy pancakes.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons white vinegar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • cooking spray

Steps

  1. Combine milk and vinegar in a medium bowl and set aside for 5 minutes to make sour milk.
  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a large mixing bowl. Whisk egg and butter into soured milk. Pour flour mixture into milk mixture and whisk until lumps are gone.
  3. Preheat a spacious skillet on medium heat, and apply a layer of cooking spray. Ladle 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet, cooking until bubbles appear and the edges become dry, typically taking 2 to 3 minutes. Use a spatula to flip the pancake, cooking until the other side turns golden brown. Repeat the process with the remaining batter.

Success! You are done!
